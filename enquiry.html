Volunteer With Us

Make a difference in the lives of rescue dogs. Join our passionate team of volunteers and help us give every dog the second chance they deserve.

Why Volunteer?

  • Make an Impact

    Your time and care directly improve the wellbeing of every dog in our shelter and foster network.

  • Meet Great People

    Join a warm and welcoming community of animal lovers who share your passion for rescue and welfare.

  • Learn New Skills

    Gain hands-on experience in animal handling, first aid, event management and community outreach.

Volunteer Requirements

  • Must be 18 years or older (16+ with parent or guardian consent)
  • Comfortable working with dogs of all sizes and temperaments
  • Complete volunteer orientation and training session
  • Reliable access to transportation
  • Pass a background check (for foster and off-site volunteers)
  • Minimum 6-month commitment

How to Apply

  1. 1

    Submit Application

    Fill out the online volunteer application form below.

  2. 2

    Interview

    A brief phone or in-person chat with our volunteer coordinator.

  3. 3

    Orientation

    Attend a training session to meet the team and learn shelter procedures.

  4. 4

    Start Volunteering

    Begin making a meaningful impact in the lives of our rescue dogs!
